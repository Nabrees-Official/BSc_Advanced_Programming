Help Guide (For New Staff)

This page explains how to use the Room Reservation System. Follow the steps below to login, add reservations, view bookings, calculate bills and logout safely.

← Back to Menu

1 Login

  • Open the system and go to the Login page.
  • Enter your username and password.
  • Click Login to access the Menu.

2 Add Reservation

  • From Menu, click Add Reservation.
  • Enter Guest Name, Address, Contact No.
  • Select Room Type and dates.
  • Click Save Reservation.

3 View Reservation

  • From Menu, click View Reservation.
  • Enter Reservation ID and click Search.
  • The system will show full reservation details.

4 Calculate Bill

  • From Menu, click Calculate Bill.
  • Enter Reservation ID.
  • Total bill is calculated using room type and number of nights.

5 View All Reservations

  • From Menu, click View All Reservations.
  • See all saved reservations.
  • Use View to open a reservation quickly.

6 Logout

  • Click Logout from the Menu when finished.
  • This closes your session securely.
Tip: Always double-check guest name, contact number, and check-in/check-out dates before saving. This helps avoid billing mistakes and booking conflicts.
